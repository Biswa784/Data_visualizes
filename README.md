# 📊 Salesforce Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

This Power BI dashboard visualizes and analyzes two years (2019–2020) of Salesforce sales data, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions. The analysis includes KPIs, trends, regional performance, and customer segmentation.

---

## 📌 Project Objectives

- Track sales performance over time
- Identify top-performing products and regions
- Understand customer behavior by segment
- Monitor delivery efficiency and payment preferences
- Detect potential churn indicators

---

## 🧩 Data Sources

- Salesforce Export (CSV/Excel)
- Cleaned and transformed in Power BI

---

## 📈 Key Visualizations

- **Sales by Category & Sub-Category**
- **Sales and Profit by State & City**
- **Monthly Sales & Profit Trends (2019-2020)**
- **Sales by Shipping Mode**
- **Sales by Customer Segment**
- **Sales by Payment Mode**
- **Top Performing Products and Regions**

---

## 📊 KPIs Tracked

| KPI                         | Description                                      |
|----------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| 📦 Total Sales             | Total revenue generated                          |
| 💰 Total Profit            | Net profit from sales                            |
| 🔢 Total Quantity          | Total items sold                                 |
| ⏱️ Avg. Delivery Time     | Average number of days to deliver an order       |
| 📈 YoY Growth %            | Year-over-year sales growth                      |
| 📊 Segment-wise Contribution | Sales distribution across Consumer, Corporate, Home Office |
| 📍 Regional Performance    | Sales and profit by state and city               |
| 🛍️ Top Products           | Highest-selling sub-categories                   |
| 🚚 Shipping Mode Impact    | Sales breakdown by shipping method               |
| 💳 Payment Mode Trends     | Usage of online, card, and COD payments          |

---

## 🚩 Insights

- **Consumer Segment** leads with 48% of sales.
- **Standard Class** is the most preferred shipping method.
- **Online payments** are used most (41%).
- **Chairs, Binders, Phones** are top sub-categories.
- Cities like **New York** and **San Francisco** dominate in sales.
- Profit and sales peak during Q4 due to holiday seasons.
- Delivery time averages **4 days** across orders.
